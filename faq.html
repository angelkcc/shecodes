Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I sign up for the Loyalty Program?

You can sign up by clicking the 'Sign Up' button on our homepage and filling out the registration form.

2. How do I earn points?

Points are earned with every purchase of seeds, fertilizers, or consultations. The more you spend, the more points you accumulate.

3. How can I redeem my points?

You can redeem points through your user dashboard by selecting available rewards.

4. Do my points expire?

Points are valid for 12 months from the date they are earned.

5. Can I transfer my points to someone else?

Currently, points are non-transferable and can only be used by the account holder.
